Temporal IK

with Kasra Ferdowsifard and James O'Brien

A technical report on the use of recurrent neural networks to predict full-body human poses in virtual reality applications. The model, built in Tensorflow, integrates with the game engine Unity to provide an end-to-end solution for VR pose estimation.

Published as my master's thesis at UC Berkeley.
